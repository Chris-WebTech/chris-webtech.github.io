About Me

I was born and raised in Kalispell, Montana. I went to Columbia Falls High School where I built my first wordPress website in 2014. After Graduating in 2019, I began serving in the United States Army as an Infantryman for 3 years. In the fall of 2023 I continued my interest in Web Technology at Flathead Valley Community College in Kalispell, MT.

Growing up in Montana there wasn't a better way to spend the time then outdoors. To this day, I still spend a lot of my free time outside. I enjoy exploring the mountains, going fishing, and being outdoors. My other free time was spent on electronics. Growing up in a generation where the computer was in your pocket always fascinated me, and when I explored for first Interactive Web class in high school I knew I wanted to be a developer. I began working towards this goal by enlisting in the Military.

Serving as an Infantryman I learned the importance of time management and attention to detail. In addition, I was recognized for various Achievements. Below is a picture of me receiving a Army Commendation Medal:

Concluding my service, I was promoted to Sergeant and lead a small team of three members. My contracted ended in October 2022.Where I returned home to the Flathead Valley and begun working as a Sales Associate for a small distribution company.

I started college in August 2023 at Flathead Valley Community College. Majoring in Web Technology, I gained foundational coding practices and got additional practice working with Wordpress, Squarespace, HTML5, as-well-as Android Studio Code for mobile applications. Integrating Python, PHP, Javascript, and SQL; I offer my abilities to help at any step in the process.
